<div class="intro-section">
  <h2>From Sargodha to UET Faisalabad</h2>

  <p>My name is Ahmad Saeed, and I come from Sargodha. My academic journey has been full of hard work, determination, and important life lessons. From my early education to starting university, every step has shaped who I am today.</p>

  <p>I performed well in my studies from the beginning. In matriculation, I secured 987 marks, which gave me confidence to continue working hard. After that, I chose ICS (Intermediate in Computer Science) because of my interest in technology and computing. During my ICS, I stayed focused on my goals and achieved 935 marks. These results encouraged me to aim higher and pursue a career in engineering.</p>

  <p>After completing my FSC, I appeared in the UET entry test. My dream was to get admission in the main campus of UET Lahore. I prepared for the test with dedication and also appeared in the second entry test to improve my chances. However, despite my efforts, I was unable to secure admission in the main campus. This was a very difficult moment for me, and I felt disappointed.</p>

  <p>But I did not give up. Instead, I accepted a new opportunity and got admission in UET Faisalabad campus in Computer Engineering. Although it was not my first choice, it was still a great achievement and the start of a new journey.</p>

  <p>Starting university life was a completely new experience. New environment, new people, and new challenges came into my life. At first, it was difficult, but gradually I adjusted and started learning. I realized that success does not depend on the campus, but on hard work and dedication.</p>

  <p>Today, I am proud to be a Computer Engineering student. This journey has taught me that failure is not the end, but a step toward success. My journey has just begun, and I am determined to achieve my goals.</p>
</div>
